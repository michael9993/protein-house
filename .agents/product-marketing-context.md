# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: March 12, 2026*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Pawzen is a modern online pet store offering curated accessories for dogs and cats.
**What it does:** Pawzen sells pet supplies (toys, bedding, grooming, collars, outdoor gear, apparel, furniture, feeding accessories) across two markets — Israel (ILS/Hebrew/RTL) and International (USD/English/LTR) — powered by the Saleor e-commerce platform with a fully configurable storefront.
**Product category:** Online Pet Store / Pet E-Commerce
**Product type:** E-commerce (physical goods, DTC + dropship hybrid via CJ Dropshipping)
**Business model:** Direct retail + dropshipping. ~130 products across 12 categories and 16 collections. Two sales channels (ILS + USD).

## Target Audience
**Target customers:** Pet owners (primarily dog and cat owners) in Israel and internationally
**Decision-makers:** Individual consumers (B2C)
**Primary use case:** Browse and buy pet accessories online with fast checkout and multi-language support
**Jobs to be done:**
- Find quality pet products without visiting physical stores
- Get pet supplies delivered to their door
- Browse by category (toys, bedding, grooming, etc.) or by pet type (dog vs cat)
**Use cases:**
- First-time pet owners outfitting a new home
- Repeat buyers restocking consumables (grooming, feeding)
- Gift shopping for pet owners
- Sale hunting / budget-conscious pet supply shopping

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Pet owners need a trustworthy, well-organized online store that's easy to browse and has fast checkout — many generic marketplaces overwhelm with options and lack pet-specific curation.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Generic marketplaces (Amazon, AliExpress) lack curation and pet-specific navigation
- Local pet stores have limited selection and no online presence
- International pet stores often don't ship to Israel or support Hebrew/RTL
**What it costs them:** Time wasted browsing through irrelevant products, paying high prices at local stores, or dealing with slow international shipping.
**Emotional tension:** Wanting the best for their pet but feeling overwhelmed by choices or uncertain about product quality.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Other online pet stores in Israel (limited), international pet e-commerce sites
**Secondary:** Amazon, AliExpress — general marketplaces with pet sections
**Indirect:** Local brick-and-mortar pet shops, veterinary clinics that sell supplies

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Bilingual Hebrew/English store with proper RTL support (rare in pet e-commerce)
- Curated collection (not overwhelming) — 130 products organized into 16 smart collections
- Modern, fast storefront with conversion-optimized checkout
- Price tier collections (Budget/Mid/Premium) for easy shopping by budget
**Why that's better:** Pet owners in Israel get a native-language shopping experience with products organized in ways that make sense for pet owners, not generic marketplaces.
